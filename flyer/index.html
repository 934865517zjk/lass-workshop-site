Submission Requirements

Authors are invited to submit original, full-length research papers that have not been published previously, accepted elsewhere, or under review.

Manuscripts must be PDF using the ACM sigconf two-column template.

  • Full papers: max 9 pages (including appendix), + unlimited refs & GenAI disclosure.
  • Appendices count toward 9‑page limit (excluding refs).
  • Double‑blind review: anonymize submissions or desk‑rejection.
  • Rejected full papers not eligible as short papers.
  • LLM‑generated text only as experimental analysis; AI tools ok for light editing.

Call for Papers

  • Alignment with Social Value
    • Environment Construction: Text, virtual, embodied, etc.
    • Agent Architecture: Memory, perception, preference, reflection, action, etc.
    • Alignment and Personalization: RLHF, offline preference optimization, inference-time alignment, multi-dimensional alignment and role play, etc.
    • Reasoning & Planning: RL from verifiable reward, multi-turn/long-horizon decision-making, etc.
  • Ethical Considerations: Ethics, risks, bias, fairness, etc.
